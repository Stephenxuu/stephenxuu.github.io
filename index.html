About Me

I'm currently pursuing my Ph.D. in Data Analytics and Decision Science at Darden School of Business, University of Virginia. I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Manel Baucells. I got my Master degree from National University of Singapore (NUS), and undergraduate degree from Central University of Finance and Economics (CUFE). Before diving into my Ph.D., I worked for one year as a Product Manager at Tencent, and another year as a Research Associate at the CUHK-Shenzhen.

My research interests include decision making under uncertainty, sequential decision making, behavioral operations management, dynamic pricing, and mechanism design.
